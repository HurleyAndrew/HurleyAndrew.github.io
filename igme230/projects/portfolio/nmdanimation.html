NMD Channel ID
A motion graphics project where I designed different tv graphics for a fictional New Media Design TV Channel.
Overview:
I used After Effects, Cinema 4D, Xparticles and Arnold Renderer to generate this series of clips for the fictional New Media Design Channel ID. The water effects, grid lines, and tenticles are all made using Xparticles. Each was rendered out in arnold and it was all compiled in After Effects.
