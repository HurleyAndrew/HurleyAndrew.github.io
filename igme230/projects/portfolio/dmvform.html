DMV Form Redesign
This project was focused on redesigning the Department of Motor Vehicles form, and the system behind the forms.
Current Design:
Overview:
The current Department of Motor Vehicles car registration form and a mess of every time of checkbox known to man. It lacks visual hierarchy and an appealing look to make the mundane process of form editing enjoyable.
Plan of Action:
Rework UX Flow

Create a Sensible Info Hierarchy

Reskin Entire Form
Interaction Reference (Turbo Tax)
This was a big inspiration for me for the layout and functioning of my compositions. The simple, understandable and relatable input structure used provides a lot of useful information for me to use. My only gripe with the form is the way things are laid out on the macro scale, such as placement of large sections.
Visual References (Various Sources)
I looked to sites like dribbble, collectui, and behance, for more of the layout inspiration as well as a few experience improvements that turbotax did not have in their product.
